About Lufthansa Airlines (LH) Flights

Lufthansa, founded in 1953, is the largest air company of Germany. It is a founding member of Star Alliance. This premium airline that commenced its operations in 1955 has its hubs at Frankfurt Airport and Munich Airport. Its headquarters are located at Cologne, Germany. Baggage Policy

Baggage allowance depends on the ticket you hold and the type of flight you're on. Baggage policy for flights within Europe will be determined by Europe Fare options. Please visit the website for further info.

Carry-on Baggage-

  • Economy & Premium Economy Class- 1 piece of carry-on baggage.
  • Business & First Class- 2 pieces of carry-on baggage.
  • Weight of the carry-on should not be more than 18 lbs.
  • Dimensions should not exceed 22x16x9 inches.
  • Along with the carry-on baggage, you may carry a personal item such as handbag, laptop bag, child's car seat, diaper bag, wheelchair etc.

Checked Baggage-

  • Economy Class- One piece of baggage that doesn't exceed the weight of 51 lbs.
  • Premium Economy Class-Two pieces of baggage is permitted. The weight of each baggage should not be more than 51 lbs.
  • Business Class- Two pieces of baggage is permitted. The weight of each should not exceed 71 lbs.
  • First Class- Three pieces of baggage is permitted. The weight of each should not exceed 71 lbs.
