Flight Details

About Spirit Airlines (NK) Flights

Spirit Airlines operates as a low-carrier US airline. It has its headquarters in Dania Beach and serves around 83 destinations globally. Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport (FLL) is the major hub for Spirit Airlines. It is listed as the seventh largest passenger carrier in North America, operating regular flights to 615 airports worldwide. On average, the airline operates more than 700 daily flights. Spirit Airlines offers domestic and international connections to the Caribbean and Latin Spirit regions.

Classes of Service

GO

Spirit Airlines offers GO as its economy seat with minimum airfare. The airline has set the lowest fare for those who need an affordable flight experience. Passengers here get checked bags and standard seat selection. In-flight amenities include Wi-Fi, snacks, and beverages. However, these amenities are not included in the air ticket fare.

Go Savvy

Go Savvy cabin seats come with an affordable price and a better onboard experience. Passengers get the choice to bring one carry-on bag or one checked bag. Standard seat selection is available while making reservations. Additional facilities such as snacks and Wi-Fi are not included.

Go Comfy

For extra comfort and ease, Go Comfy is the new seating option that offers business-class facilities to the passengers. It comes with extra amenities like snacks and drinks and benefits like priority check-in and boarding. Passengers get the option to choose a seat and carry one personal item and a carry-on bag. The first checked bag is free for Go Comfy passengers. You can expect better legroom and a blocked middle seat to add more comfort.

Go Big

Get the best in-flight experience with Go Big. With a big front seat, the airline offers a comforting journey. Passengers get spacious seating with extra legroom, additional seat cushioning, snacks and drinks that include alcoholic beverages, one carry-on bag, one checked bag, and priority check-in and boarding. For entertainment, there is WiFi available onboard.

Baggage Policy

Spirit Airlines allows passengers to carry one personal item that can fit under the seat or the overhead bins. The dimensions for personal items or bags are 18 x 14 x 8 inches.

Passengers with Go Comfy and Go Big air tickets are allowed one carry-on (22 x 18 x 10 inches) and checked luggage (maximum of 62 linear inches and 50 pounds weight). Anything exceeding the limited number, weight, and dimensions will have an additional cost. Passengers flying with GO and Go Savvy seat tickets have to pay for checked baggage.

Note that the airline does not allow luggage that exceeds 100 lb and measures more than 80 linear inches.

Web Check-in

Spirit Airlines allows passengers to save time with its web check-in service. Passengers can complete their check-in online from 24 hours up to 1 hour prior to flight departure. You can save travel time and avoid long queues at the airport with its web check-in feature. You can check in online through the official website or mobile app and get your boarding pass.
