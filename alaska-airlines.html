Flight Details

About Alaska Airlines (AS) Flights

Based in SeaTac, Washington, near Seattle, Alaska Airlines is a prominent American Carrier. It offers international and domestic services with an emphasis on the United States, Canada, Mexico, and Costa Rica. Alaska Airlines is famous in the aviation sector for its dedication to customer service, almost blemish-less on-time performance, and an extensive network along the West Coast of the U.S. Seattle-Tacoma International Airport (SEA), Portland International Airport (PDX), Los Angeles International Airport (LAX), and Anchorage International Airport (ANC) are the airline's major hubs.

Classes of Service

First Class

First class on Alaska Airlines is renowned for wide seats with leather upholstery and extra legroom space which makes traveling joyous and fun. The passengers traveling in this cabin category are entitled to premium service and complimentary meals, snacks, and beverages. Priority boarding, baggage handling, and complimentary alcoholic beverages are also some of the USPs that make first class on Alaska Airlines special.

Premium Class

Additional legroom space that allows you to stretch in comfort in your custom-designed leather seats characterizes seating in the premium class. Well-thought-out innovations like easy-to-reach cup holders perfect for holding a beer, wine, or cocktails are some of the other features that make the premium class in Alaska Airlines special.

Main Cabin (Economy)

You can expect basic services in the main cabin or economy section of Alaska Airlines. The passengers here are entitled to complimentary non-alcoholic beverages. They also have an option to purchase meals, snacks, and alcoholic beverages. Journey in the economy section of Alaska Airlines is largely an enjoyable and hassle-free affair.

Saver-Free

Saver-free resembles the economy section in many ways. The only difference is that the passengers traveling in this cabin category are offered seats at the rear of the plane. They are also the last to board the flight. They do not possess any change or cancellation facility nor are upgrades available to them.

Baggage Policy

Alaska Airlines baggage policy entitles passengers to carry-on and checked luggage according to the fare type, route, and mileage plan status. The travelers are allowed to carry 1 personal item (purse, briefcase, laptop bag) as well as 1 carry-on item for free. The carry-on items should not exceed (22 x 14 x 9in) or 115 centimeters (61 x 43 x 25 cm) including handles and wheels. The checked baggage, on the other hand, includes 2 standard bags with a maximum dimension of 62 inches or 157 centimeters (length + width + height) and an optimum weight of 50 pounds. The airline also allows for special and sports equipment. It also has a policy for transporting pets.

Web Check-In

Alaska Airlines offers a hassle-free and useful web check-in service that allows passengers to check in for their flight online and spares them the inconvenience at the airport. You can avail of the web check-in services of Alaska Airlines 24 hours before the scheduled departure of the plane. The service is active till an hour before the plane's departure. You can also receive flight updates along with the upgrade option through web check-in by logging into the Airline's website or through its mobile app.
