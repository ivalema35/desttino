Exclusive Frontier Airlines (F9) Deals & Reservations

Frontier Airlines (F9), headquartered in Denver, Colorado, United States is a premier ULCC (Ultra Low-Cost Carrier) with major hubs in Atlanta, Chicago-Midway, Chicago-O’Hare, Cincinnati, Cleveland, Dallas/Fort Worth, Denver, Las Vegas, Miami, Orlando and more. This airline operates flights to over 120 destinations in the US, Caribbean, Mexico, and Central America with a fleet size of 150 aircraft. Frontier Airlines has partnered with programs such as Marriott Bonvoy, Wyndham Rewards, and Family Pooling allowing its members to earn flyer miles and share their FRONTIER Miles with family and friends.

Classes of Service

Economy Class

Frontier Airlines operates with a single cabin class structure the economy class, allowing passengers to travel in a budget. The cabin class provides an option to enhance comfort with its standard economy seats and stretch seats. The standard seats are typical, featuring slimline design with minimal padding, offering about 28-29 inches of legroom and a width of around 18 inches. The stretch seats option offers more legroom around 36-38 inches of pitch and is located in front or exit rows of the aircraft.

Onboard Amenities

  • Food and beverages are offered on purchase to the passengers including alcohol, beer, snacks, and cocktails.
  • Passengers can add on stretch seats or bundle services for a better travel experience.
  • Onboard support for passengers with mobility challenges and special needs or for those traveling with children.

Baggage Policy

Frontier Airlines allows its passengers to carry one personal item for free. The personal item should be able to fit under the space in the front seat and should not exceed 18 x14 x8 inches. Passengers can bring one carry-on bag and two checked baggage but these are not included in the fare, passengers will have to pay a fee at the time of booking or during the check-in within 24 hours. The carry-on bag should not exceed 24in x 16in x 10in and should be able to fit in an overhead bin or under the front seat. The checked baggage should not exceed 62 linear inches and 50 lbs weight. For extra baggage, passengers will have to pay a fee.

Web Check-in

Passengers traveling with Frontier Airlines can conveniently check-in online through their website or Frontier App. It is recommended to arrive at the airport at least two hours before departure. Make sure to check in and be at the gate before cutoff time which is 60 minutes for both domestic and international flights. If passengers are not carrying any luggage and have a mobile boarding pass, then they can skip the airport line and head straight to the gate.
