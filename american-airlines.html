Flight Details

Exclusive American Airlines Deals & Reservations

American Airlines (AA) is a major US carrier that serves more than 350 destinations in 48 countries. The headquarters is in Fort Worth, and the airline has set its operating hubs in Dallas/Fort Worth, Charlotte, Chicago O'Hare, Miami, and Philadelphia. American Airlines was founded in 1926, merging eighty small airlines. Later in 2013, it merged with US Airways. Today, it is one of the largest airlines in the world, operating 6,800 flights daily with a fleet of 900+ aircraft.

AA is a full service carrier providing all cabin classes, online check-in, paid baggage, frequent flyer program and a number of other services to the passengers. Do check these details here.

Classes of Service

Basic Economy Class

Enjoy the lowest airfare with Basic Economy class. American Airlines features a basic economy cabin for a budget travel experience. Adding comfort to the journey, the cabin offers comfortable seating, free snacks, soft drinks, and inflight entertainment. The cabin ticket allows passengers to bring only one carry-on bag.

Economy Class

American Airlines Economy class offers two seat sections: Main Cabin Extra and Main Cabin. With low airfare, the cabin class allows passengers to enjoy extra legroom and comfort. Main Cabin Extra offers services such as complimentary alcoholic drinks, early boarding, a free carry-on bag, complimentary beverages (alcoholic and non-alcoholic), snacks, Wi-fi, and entertainment. Main Cabin class offers free snacks and soft drinks, in-flight entertainment, and Wi-Fi.

Premium Economy

A premium economy airline ticket of AA promises extra comfort at an affordable price. The cabin offers wider seats with extra legroom, which makes the journey relaxing. You can enjoy priority check-in and boarding. The cabin class provides in-flight chef-inspired meal service with beverages that include alcoholic and nonalcoholic options. Coming to the amenities, passengers get headphones, bedding accessories, rotating skincare products, WiFi, USB, and other required facilities.

Business Class

American Airlines has three business-class options available on different routes. Depending on the distance and flight type, passengers can book a business cabin or a flagship business class. A business ticket is available for all shorter international flights from Bermuda, Canada, the Caribbean, Mexico, Puerto Rico, and other South American cities. Get priority check-in and boarding along with flagship lounge access. In-flight service includes premium dining, Wi-Fi, inflight entertainment and more.

Flagship Business is for flights to and from Asia, Australia, Europe, New Zealand, South America, and transcontinental destinations. Passengers here get lie-flat seats, amenity kits, premium bedding, multi-course meals prepared by chefs, free entertainment, Wi-Fi, priority check-in, security and boarding, and flagship lounge access with flagship business tickets.

First Class

American Airlines first-class cabins are different for domestic flights and international flights. Domestic flights have first-class cabins; flagship first-class is available for international and transcontinental destinations. First-class cabins offer extra legroom, comfortable seats, and in-flight meals and beverages on short-haul flights. With flagship first class, passengers get priority check-in, security, and boarding facilities. Additional baggage allowance is also offered.

Baggage Policy

According to American Airlines policy, passengers can carry one free carry-on bag and a personal item or bag. The airline provides standard weight and measurement that will not extend beyond 22 x 14 x 9 inches and includes handles and wheels. If you're carrying a personal item or a bag, then it has to fit under the seat.

The baggage policy is based on destination and type of ticket or cabin class. A domestic flight ticket allows baggage weighing up to 50 pounds, and for passengers traveling by business or first-class ticket, it could go as high as 70 pounds. Premium economy, business, and first-class get up to three free check-in bags. Other than that, overweight and extra luggage are subject to additional costs.

Web Check-in

American Airlines has streamlined the boarding process for passengers with its web check-in facility. Using the mobile application or the official website, you can now complete the check-in process 24 to 1 hour before departure. No more rushing to the airport as you can easily check-in and get your boarding pass with American Airlines Web Check-in features.
